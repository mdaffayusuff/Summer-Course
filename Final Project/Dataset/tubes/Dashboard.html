Global Hunger

Hunger affects millions worldwide, with 736 million people currently facing hunger and severe food insecurity due to conflict, economic shocks, and climate change. This crisis costs the global economy an estimated $355 trillion annually in lost productivity and healthcare. Immediate and coordinated global efforts are essential to address this pressing issue and work towards the Sustainable Development Goal of Zero Hunger by 2030.

Approximately 9 million people die from hunger each year

60% of the world’s hungry live in areas affected by conflict

Hunger weakens the immune system, making individuals more susceptible to infections and diseases.
